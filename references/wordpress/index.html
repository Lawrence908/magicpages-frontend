Your Book In 4 Steps

Make bedtime magical with Magic Pages, your go-to destination for personalized children’s bedtime story books! Designed to capture your child’s imagination, each book is custom-made based on the topics your little one loves the most. Whether it’s dinosaurs, superheroes, enchanted castles, or deep-sea adventures, these custom books are crafted to bring your child’s favorite themes to life in an engaging, educational, and unforgettable way.

01 Personalize the Details

Enter your child’s name, family members’ names, and even special memories to make the stories feel truly personal. Our system weaves these details into every tale, making bedtime a magical experience.

02 Choose Your Child’s Adventure

Select the themes and topics that spark your child’s imagination—whether it’s race cars, dinosaurs, space travel, or fairytales, each chapter is built around their unique interests.

03 Let the Magic Happen

With a click, we generate a custom collection of bedtime stories designed just for your child. No pre-written templates—every book is uniquely crafted to be one-of-a-kind.

04 Receive Your Hardcover Keepsake

Your beautifully bound hardcover book is printed and shipped straight to your door. Durable, timeless, and filled with memories, it’s a bedtime treasure your child will cherish.

General Faqs

How is a Magic Pages book different from other personalized children’s books?

Unlike most personalized books that only change a name or illustration, Magic Pages books fully customize each story based on your child’s interests, family members, and even cherished memories. Every book is uniquely crafted for your child, making bedtime stories truly special.

Will my child still enjoy the book if they’re used to picture books?

Yes! Magic Pages books encourage imaginative storytelling by removing visual distractions and allowing your child to create their own mental images. This approach has been shown to enhance creativity and improve listening skills, making it a great alternative to picture books.

How long does it take to receive my book?

We produce your book within 3-5 business days after you place your order. Thanks to our global printing network, your book will ship from the closest facility to your location for fast, reliable delivery.

How do I choose the best stories for my child’s book?

Our simple order form helps guide you through the process, allowing you to select topics your child loves—like dinosaurs, race cars, or fairy tales. If you’re unsure, you can also let us curate a mix of stories based on their age and interests.

Do Magic Pages books make good gifts?

Yes! Because each book is deeply personal, they make unforgettable gifts for birthdays, holidays, and special occasions. You can even include a custom dedication to make it extra meaningful.

Will the photo I upload be used in any AI training or shared elsewhere?

Absolutely not. The image you provide is placed as is on the back cover of your child’s book and is never fed into, stored by, or trained on by any AI system. We do not share, sell, or reuse your photo for any other purpose. After your hardcover is printed, the file is archived securely and then deleted according to our privacy policy, so your family’s memories stay yours alone.
